Compression-Amplified Provenance Signal Detection for Digital Media

The signal is not the perturbation.
The signal is the system's response to the perturbation over time.

What This Is

A statistical perturbation scheme that embeds provenance signal into digital media. The signal survives and amplifies under lossy compression. It requires no infrastructure beyond the file itself.

Not a Watermark

Encodes a relationship and detects a distribution — not a visible or invisible mark.

Not Steganography

Hides nothing. The signal is a statistical property of the pixel data itself.

Not DRM

Enforces nothing. Produces measurements. Proves participation.

Not a CA

No one issues or revokes. The file carries its own evidence.

How It Works

1

Embed

At save time, force inter-channel distances at selected pixel positions to prime-valued gaps. Cost: ~0.01 joules. Imperceptible to the human eye.

2

Compress

The codec's block-based quantization penalizes the perturbation's local complexity. Each generation amplifies the variance anomaly at marker positions.

3

Detect

Measure statistical distributions at candidate vs. control positions. The image is its own control group. No reference image needed.

4

Attribute

Positions derived from a 256-bit key via HMAC-SHA512. The position pattern is the fingerprint. C(4000,200) ≈ 10400 possible patterns.

The Fuse

The prime values. Destroyed by the first compression. Engineering: format-specific, replaceable.

The Fire

The variance anomaly. Persists and amplifies. Physics: universal to any partition-transform-quantize system.

Torture Tests

We tried to kill it. Here's what happened.

↻

Rotation Attack

SURVIVED
  • Flips: perfect detection (3.3x ratio, p≈10-42)
  • Up to 7° rotation: detected without position correction
  • Full chain (rotate + flip + rotate + flip + JPEG): 4.0x ratio
  • Round-trip inverse: detected despite 97.5% pixel damage
  • Fingerprint Jaccard: 0.72 after full chain
✂

Slice & Stitch

SURVIVED
  • Image sliced into 4 quadrants, each re-encoded JPEG Q85
  • Individual fragments: detection breaks (too few markers)
  • Stitched back: detection returns (3.6x, p=0.001)
  • Stitched + JPEG Q75: still detected (3.4x, p=0.003)
  • Stitch seams forensically visible (3.3x seam ratio)

"The attack undoes itself on reassembly."

⤢

Scale & Resize

SURVIVED
  • 1024px: strong detection through full cascade (3.4x→2.5x)
  • 2048px: amplifying variance ratio 3.7 → 9.3
  • Resize to 50%, 25%, even 12%: detected
  • Resize + WebP transcoding: detected at all sizes
  • Fingerprint Jaccard >0.47 across all transforms

Five Detection Layers

Defense in depth. Each layer operates independently in a different domain, with a validated operational range. Together they compound.

A

DQT Prime Tables

Replace JPEG quantization table entries with nearest primes. The table itself is the provenance signal. Container-level detection. Operational: G0 only — by design, the container signal does not survive re-encoding.

BC

Compound Frequency Markers

Twin-prime markers at known positions. Detection compares prime-gap hit rate at marker vs. control positions in the same image. Compound strategies collapse false positive probability. Operational: G0–G2 — frequency signal degrades under aggressive compression. 96.4% G-B detection at Q95, not reliable past Q60.

D

Blind Spatial Variance

KS test on local spatial variance and channel-difference variance distributions. The image is its own control group. No reference image needed. Compression amplifies the signal — the adversary who compresses to destroy the frequency signal is constructing the spatial variance detection signal. Operational: G0–G4 — 90%+ blind detection at Q40 on 500 images. This is the paper.

E

Spanning Relational Sentinel

Mersenne-prime anchors with correlated flanking pixels within 8×8 DCT blocks. The differential between positions survives when absolute values do not. Tiered detection (24/16/8-bit) degrades gracefully at block boundaries. Operational: G0–G4 — 98.6% manifest survival at Q40, >99% effective at any tier. Validated on 500 images.

F

Rare Basket / Attribution

Position pattern derived from 256-bit key via HMAC-SHA512. Enables attribution through Jaccard similarity at corpus scale. The fingerprint, not the signal. Payload encoder (structured flanking data: creator ID, timestamp, section index) is architecturally ready but not yet validated at scale.

Beyond JPEG

The fuse is engineering. The fire is physics.

Any codec that uses a partition → transform → quantize pipeline is susceptible. The mechanism is universal:

Images

JPEG, WebP, HEIF — all use block-based DCT or wavelet quantization. Tested and confirmed across JPEG and WebP with cross-codec transcoding.

Audio

MP3, AAC, Opus — frequency-domain quantization in sub-band partitions. Initial MP3 testing has exceeded expectations.

Video

H.264, H.265, AV1 — block-based motion-compensated DCT quantization. The same thermodynamic tax applies per-frame.
